## Ingredients

1 c peanut butter
1 c margarine or shortening
1 c granulated sugar
1 c firmly packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2.5 c unsifted flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
~60-70 Chocolate kisses, unwrapped

## Instructions

Mix peanut butter, margarine/shortening, sugar, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla on medium speed. Mix in the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.

Shape into 1" balls using the cookie ball scoop.

Roll balls in granulated sugar.

Place 2" apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake at 350°F for 12-15 minutes (9 minutes if convection).

Remove cookies, place on paper, and immediately press kisses into cookies. Cool on paper.

Store in an airtight container.
