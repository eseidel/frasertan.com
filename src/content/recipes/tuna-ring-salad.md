## Instructions

A beloved Seidel family tradition!

2 cans chunk light tuna5 ounces small sized pasta (this appears to be a regional thing. Some places don’t sell small rings, so use small shells)1 medium onion, chopped1 cup chopped celery5 hard boiled eggs, cut upDrain tuna and mix with juice of one lemon.Mix everything together and add dressing:6 or so overfilled tablespoons of mayonnaise, to taste. (I usually use lite. )Lots of fresh pepperLawry’s or other seasoned saltgarlic powder to taste

From Peggy
