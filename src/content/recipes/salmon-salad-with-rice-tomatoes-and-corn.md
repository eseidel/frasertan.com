## Ingredients

1 cup jasmine rice, uncooked
kosher salt
1 ear yellow corn
1/3 English cucumber, cut into 1/4" slices, roughly 1 cup
1 vine-ripened tomato, cut into 1/4" dice (1 cup)
2 tbsp chives or green onions, 1/2 lengths
2 cups small lettuce varietals (like Little Gems)
1/2 lb wild salmon fillets (2" thick), deboned
black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil + 3 tbsp for dressing
2 tbsp champagne vinegar
Pinch of sugar or honey to taste

## Instructions

Cook the rice with a bit of salt. Fluff with fork, let rest 5 minutes.

Steam corn 3-5 minutes. Remove kernels with a sharp knife. Place in bowl with tomato, cucumber, chives, lettuce and rice.

Season salmon well with salt and pepper. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a nonstick pan over medium-high heat until hot. Add the salmon flesh side down adn sear for 4 minutes per side. Let rest 5 minutes.

Place vinegar in a small bowl and whisk with salt, pepper and honey. Whisk oil into vinegar mix. Toss rice and veggies with dressing. Flake salmon into pieces and add, gently tossing. Serve.

## Notes

- This is EVEN BETTER with grilled salmon _swoon_.
