## Ingredients

1 cup heavy whipping cream, chilled
1/4 cup sour cream, chilled
2 tbsp brown sugar
1/8 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

Whip all ingredients together on medium-low until foamy. Increase speed to medium-high and whip until soft peaks form.
