## Ingredients

1 lb flank steak
1 3/4 tsp salt, divided
3/4 tsp pepper, divided
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tbsp water
1 tbsp lime juice, with wedges for serving
1 tbsp minced chipotles in adobo
1 large head romaine lettuce, halved lengthwise then sliced thin
1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed
1 avocado, pitted and chunked
2 oz tortilla chips, coarsely crushed (about 1 cup)
Equipment

## Instructions

Pat steak dry, then sprinkle with 1 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until just smoking. Add steak and cook until medium-rare, about 5-7 minutes per side. Set aside and tent for 5 minutes, then slice into bite sized pieces against the grain

Whisk together sour cream, water, lime juice, chipotle (1 tbsp is not too much, it's muted by the sour cream!), 3/4 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper.

Add lettuce, beans, avocado and tortilla chips to a bowl. Toss with dressing and top with steak.
