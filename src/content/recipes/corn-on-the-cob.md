## Instructions

Corn on the cob is the best thing in the world!! Here are a few easy ways to cook it.

Grilled corn on the cob:Toss the unshucked corn on a hot grill. Grill for 20 minutes, rotating 90 degrees every 5 minutes. Boiled corn:Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the schucked corn, cover, and turn off the hear. Let sit at least 10minutes and up to 30 minutes until ready to nosh!
