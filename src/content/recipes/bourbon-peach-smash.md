## Ingredients

Peach shrub:
1 large very ripe peach, pitted, very thinly sliced
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 to 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
Pinch of salt
Assembly:
A few macerated peach slices
3 to 4 fresh mint leaves
Ice
2 tablespoons (1 ounce) syrup from peaches
3 tablespoons (1 1/2 ounces) bourbon
Splash of chilled seltzer or ginger beer

## Instructions

Prepare the peach shrub: Combine the peach slices, sugar, water and 1-2 tablespoons of vinegar in a lidded jar. Let steep for at least 3 hours in the fridge; it will keep up to a week.

To assemble the drink: Use a fork to pull a few peach slices out of the jar and add to the bottom of a glass. Muddle with a few mint leaves (if you like mint).

Add 2 tablespoons of the syrup from the peaches and 3 tablespoons of bourbon. Add ice cubes and stir.

Add seltzer or ginger beer to taste.
