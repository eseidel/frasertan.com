## Ingredients

1 cup butter, softened
1 8 oz package cream cheese, room temperature
1 clove garlic, minced
0.5 tsp oregano
0.5 tsp dried basil
0.125 tsp each dill, marjoram, thyme and pepper
OR
0.5 tsp poultry seasoning

## Instructions

Stir all ingredients to combine, then chill.
