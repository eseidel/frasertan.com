## Ingredients

4 cups water or vegetable broth
1.5 tsp instant dashi
4 tbsp shiro miso
wakame
chopped scallions
firm tofu, cubed

## Instructions

Heat the water and dashi granules over medium high heat until boiling. Reduce heat to medium and add the tofu to warm. Using about 0.5 c of the broth, add to the miso in a small bowl and mix to loosen the miso paste. Add back to broth and stir to mix. Off heat, add scallions and wakame. Serve.

## Notes

- Don't let the miso boil.
