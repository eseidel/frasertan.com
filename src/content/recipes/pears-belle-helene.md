## Ingredients

Pears:
1 cup sugar
1 cup white wine
3 cups water
zest of one orange, cut into 1" x 3" strips
1 cinnamon stick
8 whole cloves
2 tsp vanilla extract
6 pears, preferably slightly underripe Boscs, peeled
Chocolate sauce:
1/3 cup heavy cream
1/3 cup liqueur of choice
1/4 cup sugar
pinch of salt
8 oz semisweet chocolate chips
vanilla ice cream

## Instructions

To poach the pears, combine all the ingredients and bring to boil. Add the pears and simmer for 20 minutes or so, until tender. Remove from the poaching liquid and chill until cool.

Combine the heavy cream, liqueur, sugar and salt in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted and smooth.

Serve a generous dollop of vanilla ice cream alongside the pear, and drizzle liberally with chocolate sauce.
