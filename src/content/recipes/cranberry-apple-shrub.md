## Ingredients

3 medium apples, quartered (no need to peel or core)
1 cup cranberries
1 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup turbinado sugar
Equipment
non-reactive 4 cup vessel
fine mesh strainer

## Instructions

Shred the apples, either on a box grater or using a food processor.

Meanwhile, add cranberries and apple cider vinegar to a blender and blend until pureed.

Add shredded apples, cranberry vinegar mixture and sugar to a non-reactive container. Cover and leave in a cool countertop place for 1-2 days.

Strain apple cranberry mixture, squeezing on solids to remove as much liquid as possible. Discard solids.

Store liquid in a clean mason jar or glass bottle; shake well to combine.

## Notes

- Shrub should keep well for up to 1 year.
- Unlike my store bought vinegars, it seems to take a lot more of this homemade shurb to make a balance i like: I used 6 tbsp in 12 oz San Pellegrino.
