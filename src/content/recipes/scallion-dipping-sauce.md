## Ingredients

1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons rice vinegar
2 tablespoons mirin
2 tablespoons water
1 teaspoon chili oil, (optional)
1/2 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
1 medium scallion, white and green parts, minced

## Instructions

Combine all ingredients in bowl and serve.
