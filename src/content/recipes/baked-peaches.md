## Ingredients

1 peach, split in half, pit removed
1 tbsp brown sugar
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
honey
heavy cream

## Instructions

Place each peach half in a snug fitting ramekin. Sprinkle with vanilla and drizzle with honey. Sprinkle with brown sugar.

Bake in a 350°F oven for 20-30 minutes.

Pour in a little cream if wanted and eat!
