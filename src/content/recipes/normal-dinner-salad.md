## Ingredients

1 head read leaf lettuce
2 apples
2 bell peppers (not green)
8 oz fresh mozzarella balls (ciliegene)
Balsamic Vinaigrette

## Instructions

1. Rip the lettuce into bite sized pieces and wash thoroughly. Dry with a salad spinner. (No one likes grit in their teeth!)

2. Chop the apples into small chunks. Do the same with the peppers. Add to the lettuce.

3. Cut the mozzarella balls in half. Sprinkle over salad.

4. Serve with vinaigrette on the side!
