## Ingredients

1 cup milk
4 eggs + 1 egg yolk
1 tbsp sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp cinnamon
6 cups cubed bread, tightly packed

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°. Grease a squares pan or a regular muffin tin or an 8" square cake pan.

Whisk together the eggs and yolk. Heat the milk in the microwave for 1 minute, then add into the eggs. Add in the sugar, vanilla and cinnamon and whisk to combine well.

Add the bread cubes and toss to combine; let the bread absorb the liquid.

Spoon into the prepared pan, then bake for 15-20 minutes (muffins) or 30 minutes (cake pan), until a toothpick come out clean. If the tops start to brown too much, cover with tinfoil.

## Notes

- The original recipe calls for 4 cups bread, but I found that too eggy. Alternatively, you could reduce the liquid amount.
- Use a nice fluffy white bread or brioche or challah.
