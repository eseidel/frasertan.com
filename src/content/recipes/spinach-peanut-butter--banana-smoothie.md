## Ingredients

1 cup plain kefir
1 tablespoon peanut butter
1 cup spinach
1 frozen banana
1 tablespoon honey (optional)
protein powder (optional)
Equipment
Blender

## Instructions

Blend all ingredients until smooth.

## Notes

- No, you really can't taste the spinach.
- I don't consider the honey optional :P
- I add in 2 tbsp Vital Proteins unflavored collagen powder.
