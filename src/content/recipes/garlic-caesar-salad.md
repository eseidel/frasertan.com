## Ingredients

1 egg
1 garlic clove (I added one more cause i like garlic)
Pinch and a half salt
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp olive oil
1 to 2 bunches romaine lettuce, washed and shredded
1-2 tbsp Parmesan cheese
Croutons

## Instructions

Get out the salad bowl. Sprinkle the salt in the bottom. Peel and lightly crush the garlic. Rub the salt in the bowl with the garlic, then, using a fork, crush up the garlic with the salt. Add the mustard, lemon juice and olive oil and whisk together well. add the romaine lettuce and toss well.

Bring a small pot of water to a boil. Add the egg and let boil for 1 minute, then remove.

Sprinkle parmesan over lettuce and croutons, then crack egg into salad. Toss well and serve.
