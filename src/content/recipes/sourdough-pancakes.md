## Ingredients

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tbsps sugar
1/2 tsp fine sea salt
2 tsps baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup sourdough starter (discard or active)
2 large eggs
1 cup milk, plus more as needed
3 tbsps melted unsalted butter or oil, plus more for skillet

## Instructions

Combine flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in a large mixing bowl. Add sourdough starter, eggs, milk, and melted butter. Whisk gently until smooth; some lumps are fine.

Melt a small pat of butter in a cast iron or non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and swirl to coat.

Pour 1/3 cup batter into the pan. Cook 1-2 minutes until edges puff and bubbles appear on the surface, then flip. Cook another minute.

Wipe the skillet between batches with a paper towel and add more butter as needed. Repeat until batter is finished.

Serve warm with maple syrup, fresh berries, and powdered sugar.
