## Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsps baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
2 tbsps granulated sugar
1 tsp salt
1 cup sourdough discard (room temperature)
1 1/2 cups 2% milk (room temperature)
1 large egg, beaten (room temperature)
2 tbsps vegetable oil

## Instructions

Whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.

Add sourdough discard, milk, egg, and oil atop the dry mixture. Mix until just combined; some lumps are acceptable to prevent overdeveloping the batter.

Grease a griddle or cast iron skillet with butter or oil. Heat over medium-low.

Pour 1/3 cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form on top, then gently flip.

Cook the second side 1-2 minutes until golden brown and fully cooked.

Serve warm with butter and maple syrup.
