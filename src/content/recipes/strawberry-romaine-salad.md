## Ingredients

1 cup veg oil
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. ground white pepper
1 large head romaine lettuce
1 head Boston lettuce or spinach
1 pint strawberries, hulled and sliced
1 cup (4 oz) shredded Monterey Jack cheese or mild cheddar
1/2 cup chopped walnuts, toasted

## Instructions

Combine dressing in a jar and shake vigorously. Tear up lettuces, combine with strawberries, cheese and walnuts in a large bowl.

Pour dressing over salad and toss.

Keep extra dressing in the refrigerator.

## Notes

- This make a lot of dressing! You can probably halve that part easily.
