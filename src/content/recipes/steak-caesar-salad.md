## Ingredients

1.5 lbs sirloin steak tips, trimmed and cut into 2 inch chunks
3 tbsp Worchestershire sauce, divided
2 3/4 tsp pepper, divided
1 1/2 tsp salt, divided
4 oz ciabatta, cut into 1/2 inch chunks
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil, divided
1 tbsp garlic, mined (or more!)
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tbsp lemon juice
3 heads romaine lettuce, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
Equipment

## Instructions

Adjust oven rack position to middle and preheat oven to 350°.

Toss steak with 2 tbsp Worchestershire sauce, 2 tsp pepper and 3/4 tsp salt; set aside.

Toss bread with 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 tsp garlic, 1/4 tsp pepper and 1/4 tsp salt on a rimmed baking sheet. Bake until light golden, 16 to 18 minutes, stirring once halfway through.

Heat tablespoon of oil in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add steak and cook until browned on all sides and meat registers 120°-125° (2-3 minutes per side). Set steak aside and tent with foil.

Whisk together Parmesan, mayo, lemon juice, remaining 1 tbsp Worchestershire, 1/2 tsp pepper, 1/2 tsp salt, 3 tbsp olive oil and remaining tsp garlic.

In a large bowl, toss together lettuce, tomatoes and croutons. Serve, passing steak and dressing alongside.

## Notes

- This is the best non-anchovy Caesar dressing!!
- I will also do this with flank steak either in a pan or - easy peasy! - on the grill!
