## Ingredients

Half a head of green cabbage
one bunch of chard
garlic cloves (as many as you like!)
1/2 cup chicken broth
olive oil
salt and pepper

## Instructions

Preheat your oven to 350°.

Remove the stems of the chard; roughly chop and toss into a big baking dish. Tear the leaves into bite sized pieces and add to the stems. Roughly chop the cabbage and add to the chard. Thinly slice the garlic and toss it in.

Pour a generous amount of olive oil over the veggies, then pour in the chicken broth. Use tongs to toss gently.

Cover the dish with foil and bake for 15 minutes to start steaming things. Then, remove the foil, season with salt and pepper, and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, until the cabbage is to your liking. Serve with sausages!

## Notes

- This is a pretty free-form recipe; feel free to change up the ingredients as you want!
