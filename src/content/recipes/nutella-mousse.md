## Ingredients

4 oz (1/2 package) cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup Nutella
1 cup heavy whipping cream

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix together the softened cream cheese and Nutella until well blended.

2. Slowly add the cream and mix into the cream cheese nutella mixture until well blended and kinda soupy.

3. Using an electric mixer, whip to soft peaks.

4. Divide into ramekins and chill in the fridge to set.
