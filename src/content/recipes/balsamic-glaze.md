## Ingredients

1 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup brown sugar

## Instructions

Combine the vinegar and the sugar in a small saucepan over medium heat. Stir to dissolve the sugar.

Simmer until reduced to about 1/3 to 1/4 cup and mixture become thick and syrupy.

Store in the fridge.
