## Ingredients

1 lb spaghetti or rice noodles
0.75 c chunky peanut butter
0.25 c lime juice
0.25 c coconut milk
3 tbsp soy sauce
4 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp honey
3 tbsp water
chili paste
garlic powder
onion powder
ginger powder
cilanto
peanuts

## Instructions

Boil pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.

Combine peanut butter, lime juice, coconut milk, soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, water and spices (to taste) in a small saucepan and cook over low heat until combined and slightly thickened.

Pour over pasta and mix thoroughly. Garnish with cilantro and peanuts and serve.
