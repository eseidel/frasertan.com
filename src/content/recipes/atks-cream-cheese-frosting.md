## Ingredients

1 1/4 pounds cream cheese, softened
12 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened
2 tablespoons sour cream
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 1/2 cups (10 ounces) confectioner's sugar
Equipment

## Instructions

In a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat cream cheese, butter, sour cream, vanilla and salt on medium speed until smooth, about 2 minutes. Reduce speed to slow, slowly add the sugar, and beat until incorporated, about 4 minutes.

Increase speed ot medium-high and beat until soft and fluffy, about 4 minutes.

## Notes

- From ATK:Do not use low fat cream cheese, as the frosting will end up too soupy to useThis frosting comes out softer and looser than other frostingsFrosting can be refrigerated for up to 3 days - let soften at room temperature for about 1 hour then rewhip on medium speed until smooth, about 2 minutes. To make 3 cups frosting, reduce cream cheese to 12 ounces, butter to 6 tablespoons, sour cream to 1 1/2 tablespoons, vanilla to 1 teaspoon and sugar to 1 1/2 cups (6 ounces).
- Do not use low fat cream cheese, as the frosting will end up too soupy to use
- This frosting comes out softer and looser than other frostings
- Frosting can be refrigerated for up to 3 days - let soften at room temperature for about 1 hour then rewhip on medium speed until smooth, about 2 minutes.
- To make 3 cups frosting, reduce cream cheese to 12 ounces, butter to 6 tablespoons, sour cream to 1 1/2 tablespoons, vanilla to 1 teaspoon and sugar to 1 1/2 cups (6 ounces).
