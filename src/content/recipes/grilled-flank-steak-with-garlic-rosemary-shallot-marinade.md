## Ingredients

6 tbsp olive oil
6 medium garlic cloves, minced (about 2 tbsp)
1 medium shallot, minced (about 3 tbsp)
2 tbsp minced fresh rosemary
1 flank steak (about 2 lbs)
2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
Equipment

## Instructions

Puree all marinade ingredients in a blender until smooth, scraping down as needed.

Place steak on rimmed baking sheet or in shallow baking dish. Using a dinner fork, prick the steak about 20 times on each side. Rub both sides of the steak even with salt, then with marinade paste. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 1 hour and up to 24.

Heat gas grill to high.

Using paper towels, wipe the marinade off the steak. Season both sides of the steak with pepper. Grill until well browned on first tide, 4 to 6 minutes. Flip, and cook until second side if well browned, another 3 to 4 minutes. Check if the steak is done to medium rare - if not, cook an additional 1-2 minutes. Do not overcook, as steak will continue to cook while it rests.

Transfer steak to clean cutting board and tent loosely with foil. Let rest 5 to 10 minutes. Carve the steak into 1/4" slices against the grain and serve.

## Notes

- Flank steaks smaller than 2 pounds can be used, but adjust down the amount of salt and pepper accordingly.
- If using a gas grill, follow the times in Step 3 but keep the cover down.
