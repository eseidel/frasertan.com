## Ingredients

Salad
2-3 cups cooked couscous
6 green onions, chopped
2 medium sized tomatoes, peeled seeded and diced small
1 cup cucumber, peeled and diced small
1/2 cup chickpeas or black-eyed peas, canned/cooked
1 cup chopped fresh parsley or baby spinach
Dressing:
3/4 cup olive oil
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp dijon mustard
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp salt
black pepper to tase
Equipment

## Instructions

Toss all ingredients together with dressing.
