## Ingredients

1 lime, cut into 8 slices
2 shots cachaca
2 tsps sugar
club soda

## Instructions

Muddle 2 slices of lime with the sugar. Add several ice cubes.

Add cachaca. Top with club soda or fizzy mineral water to taste.
