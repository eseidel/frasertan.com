## Instructions

To make the croutons: Cut the loaf into generous 1 1/2" slices. Remove the crusts and discard (or turn into breadcrumbs). Cut slices into generous 1 1/2" chunks. Rustic shapes are good here.

Melt the butter (with the salt and garlic powder if using), then toss with the bread cubes to coat.

Toast in a 375° oven for 10-15 minutes, flipping occasionally, until golden and toasty on all sides.

Drain on a paper-towel lined plate.

Serve with salad!

## Notes

- I usually make this a hearty dinner salad, not a side salad, so I pile on the fixings.
